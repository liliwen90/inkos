## Narrative Perspective
Third-person limited, closely following Qin Feng. The narrative voice should be grounded and descriptive, leaning into the technical aspects of mech operation and the visceral, physical sensations of piloting. When describing cultivation processes or Spiritual Sense, use metaphors rooted in engineering and physics ("the Qi circulated like a superconductor," "his awareness diffused through the mech like a penetrating scan") to maintain the sci-fi tone. Internal monologue is key for showing his unique perspective.

## Core Conflict Driver
The central tension is between **Revelation and Concealment**. Every use of Qin's power risks exposure, which could lead to capture, dissection, or forced servitude. His progression is therefore a double-edged sword: getting stronger is necessary for survival and vengeance, but each new ability or stage makes him harder to hide. This drives a constant need for ingenuity—how can he achieve his goals while making his power look like something else (extreme talent, hacked tech, unknown prototype)?
